Who We Are

HireLocal is on a mission to reduce youth unemployment in South Africa through technology

Bridging the Employment Gap

Founded in 2022 in response to South Africa's crippling youth unemployment crisis, HireLocal connects young job seekers with businesses needing reliable, on-demand talent.

Our platform makes it easy for businesses to find vetted local workers for short-term gigs, while providing youth with accessible opportunities to earn income and gain experience.

We believe every young South African deserves access to dignified work opportunities, and every business should be able to find quality local talent quickly and easily.
